German irregular verbs

11 August 2021 Detailed data Verbs German

Here are some common German irregular verbs. We list respectively the infinitive, third person singular present, third person singular simple past, third person singular perfect. Some verbs which originally form perfect with ist , can also go with hat now, and they go with hat when used in the transitive sense. Some verbs which are traditionally strong, have also allowed weak conjugation.
