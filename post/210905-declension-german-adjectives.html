Declension of German adjectives

5 September 2021 Negligible notes Adjectives German

At first glance, the declension of German adjective seems hard to remember. There are three cases: strong declension (without any article), mixed declension (with ein, kein, mein, dein, ihr and so on), and weak declension (with der, welcher, solcher, dieser, aller and so on). However, not all of them has to be memorized.

In strong declension, adjectives agree with the suffix of the supposed article.

Strong declension
